El pibe de Fiorito

(The boy from Fiorito)

Esta pequeña serie de ilustraciones fue inspirada por una frase del usuario @Tano2412 de Twitter. En medio del duelo nacional, encontré esta manera de rendir homenaje y expresar mi amor a Diego Armando Maradona. Que lo disfruten.

This small series of illustrations was inspired by a phrase from Twitter user @Tano2412. In the middle of the national mourning, I found this way to pay tribute and express my love to Diego Armando Maradona. Enjoy it.

One cold winter night, in the humble neighborhood of Villa Fiorito, Argentina, a boy sleeps with his little siblings.

So that he would not get sick, God touched his left foot, putting it under the blanket.

That caress created the magic that would become the revenge of the humble against power.
